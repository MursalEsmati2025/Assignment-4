    About Afghan Preverbs

    Afghan proverbs are an essential part of the culture, reflecting the wisdom, humor, and experiences of the people. These short sayings are often used in daily conversations to offer advice or explain life situations. They carry valuable lessons and help preserve traditions, passing down knowledge through generations.




    Featured Proverbs

    صبر تلخ است ولی بر شیرین دارد

    Patience is bitter, but it has sweet consequences.

    ♦️ This proverb teaches us that patience can ultimately bring us satisfaction and achieve desired results.


    کوه به کوه نمیرسد ،آدم به آدم میرسد…

    Mountains do not meet, but people do...

    ♦️ People often cross paths again, even when we least expect it. Life has a strange way of bringing us back to those we once knew. That’s why it’s always wise to treat everyone with kindness and respect. A small act of goodness can leave a lasting impact, and you never know when you’ll meet someone again.
